About bliss.

Welcome to our site, your home for mindfulness and yoga exercises. Our site was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to help people find peace and balance in their busy lives.

Our purpose

As our founder, Carl Murray, experienced burnout and stress in his own life, he recognized the need for accessible, effective tools for promoting mindfulness and relaxation. He turned to yoga and meditation as a source of healing and rejuvenation, and saw firsthand the transformative power of these practices.

With a passion for sharing these tools with others, he set out to create a space where people from all walks of life could access high-quality yoga and mindfulness exercises, without needing to attend a physical class or retreat. Today, we are proud to offer a range of yoga and mindfulness exercises to suit every need, from quick meditations for busy days to longer yoga flows for deep relaxation.

Our team of experienced teachers and practitioners is dedicated to supporting you on your journey towards greater mindfulness, calm, and balance. We believe that everyone deserves access to the tools they need to live their best life, and we are committed to providing a space where people can come together to learn, grow, and connect.

Thank you for joining us on this journey, and we look forward to supporting you every step of the way.
